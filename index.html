Introduction

Dungeons and & Dragons, commonly abbreviated as D&D, is a role-playing game.
Originally invented in 1974 by Gary Gygax as a way to tell group stories, it is now
played all aross the world.
This site will give brief history of the game,
overveiw what might occur in a play session, and provide details and links to get or use the game.
